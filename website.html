Why Does This Matter?

We need a solution to save your neighborhood and community from flooding. Every year New York City is at risk, and there are no real solutions to mitigate this. This website is meant to educate and provide community members with a way to share their thoughts.

Green Infrastructure

Green infrastructure is all around us already. But it is unevely divided. Do you see that large green circle at the top of this chart? Yes, that's Central Park. This area serves Manhattan, but is not necessary accesible for the greater New York City area. That is why it is important to implement more accessible green infrastructure. We can do this by introducing more local parks.
